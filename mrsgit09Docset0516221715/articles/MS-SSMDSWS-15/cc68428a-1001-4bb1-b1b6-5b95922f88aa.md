<p><b>CommitVersion</b>: Specifies whether the version
that is validated is to be committed after validation.</p>

<p><b>ReturnChangedMemberIds</b>: Specifies whether to
return the identifiers of members that are changed during the validation
process.</p>

<p><b>ReturnValidationResults</b>: Specifies whether the
validation results are to be returned after validation.</p>

<p><b>ValidateEntityAsync</b>: Specifies whether the
server is to perform entity validations asynchronously.</p>
