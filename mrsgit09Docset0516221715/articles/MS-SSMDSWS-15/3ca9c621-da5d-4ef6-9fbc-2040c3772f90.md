<p><b>ChangesetId</b>: Specifies the identifier
associated with a given change set.</p>

<p><b>EntityId</b>: Specifies the identifier of the
entity for the contained members.</p>

<p><b>MemberType</b>: Specifies the type of member
record within an entity.</p>

<p><b>Members</b>: Specifies the list of members for the
identified entity and member type.</p>

<p><b>ModelId</b>: Specifies the identifier of the model
for the contained members.</p>

<p><b>VersionId</b>: Specifies the identifier of the
version for the contained members.</p>
