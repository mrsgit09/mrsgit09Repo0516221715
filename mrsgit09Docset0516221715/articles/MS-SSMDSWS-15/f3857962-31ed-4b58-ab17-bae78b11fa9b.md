<p><b>AuditInfo</b>: Specifies the modification history
of the change set.</p>

<p><b>Description</b>: Specifies the string that
represents the long description of the change set.</p>

<p><b>EntityId</b>: Specifies the identifier of the
entity.</p>

<p><b>Identifier</b>: Specifies the identifier of the
change set.</p>

<p><b>Status</b>: Specifies the status of the change
set.</p>
