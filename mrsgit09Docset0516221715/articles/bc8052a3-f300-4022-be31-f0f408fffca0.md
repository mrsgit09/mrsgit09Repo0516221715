<p><b>btkey (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> The
key value associated with this BTENTRY. All the entries in the child BTPAGE
referenced by <b>BREF</b> have key values greater than or equal to this key
value. The <b>btkey</b> is either an NID (zero extended to 8 bytes for Unicode
PSTs) or a BID, depending on the <b>ptype</b> of the page.</p>

<p><b>BREF (Unicode: 16 bytes; ANSI: 8 bytes):</b> <b>BREF</b>
structure (section <a href="844a5ebf-488a-45fd-8fce-92a84d8e24a3.htm">2.2.2.4</a>)
that points to the child BTPAGE.</p>
