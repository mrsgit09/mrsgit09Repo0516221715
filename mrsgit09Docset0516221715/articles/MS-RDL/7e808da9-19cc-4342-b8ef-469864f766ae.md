<p><b><i>Applies to </i></b><a href="3ebe2912-4958-4832-b391-cad1f5e13338.htm"><b><i>RDL 2005/01</i></b></a><b><i>,

<p>The <b>CustomProperty</b> element specifies the contents of
a <b>CustomProperty</b> that is passed to rendering and to <a href="6bb7b35c-e517-4444-a96b-9f2ccdd1a642.htm">CustomReportItem</a>
components. This element MUST be specified at least once within a <a href="93994776-7d8e-4cf2-932f-9c085f3deaf8.htm">CustomProperties</a>
collection.</p>
