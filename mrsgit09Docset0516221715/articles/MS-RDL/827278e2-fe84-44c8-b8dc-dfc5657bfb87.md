of a <a href="afff0921-7d95-4216-8f28-635c67d539d8.html">PageSection</a>. This
element MUST be specified, and its value MUST be an <a href="b40c092e-4fe5-4f7b-a0bf-c98df1361c90.html">RdlSize</a>. </p>
