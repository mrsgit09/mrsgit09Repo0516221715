that will occur if a <a href="b0ab5524-7eb2-47a7-a4d3-230f5c8c5526.html">Chart</a>

elements or <a href="933ac037-f12b-45de-8731-cb24c952ea7a.html">CategoryGrouping.StaticCategories</a>
