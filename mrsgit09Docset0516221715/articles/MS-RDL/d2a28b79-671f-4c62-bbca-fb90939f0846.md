displayed on top of a <a href="021b569b-07ae-462a-ac62-d3ab51f183f5.htm">LinearGauge</a>
or a <a href="2e113607-ee33-4abd-9ae3-6607c10d3c8a.htm">RadialGauge</a>. This
element is optional. </p>
