and </i></b><a href="52ce3983-2bfc-4e72-9359-42aaf5fe4509.html"><b><i>RDL 2016/01</i></b></a></p>

<p>The <b>GaugeLabel</b> element specifies a label to display
within a <a href="f01744d3-79fa-4f30-94bf-a1ffa6bde2ac.html">GaugePanel</a>
instance. The <b>GaugeLabel</b> element MUST be specified at least once within
a <a href="1d876044-b790-4413-a225-e60f7d727465.html">GaugeLabels</a>
collection.</p>
