catalog.</p>

</li><li><p><span><span> 
</span></span>ReportService2006 <mshelp:link keywords="eea1faab-ab5f-4fac-aecd-5c7543a8977c" tabindex="0">[MS-RSWSRMSM2006]</mshelp:link>:

catalog.</p>
