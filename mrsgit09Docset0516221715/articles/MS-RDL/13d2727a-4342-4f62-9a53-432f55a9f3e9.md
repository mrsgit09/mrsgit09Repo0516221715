<p><b><i>Applies to </i></b><a href="1e855f94-4617-47e4-b89e-0856c6cb420f.html"><b><i>RDL 2008/01</i></b></a><b><i>,
</i></b><a href="3428e690-a348-4ec7-8a6a-8efb42d2cdee.html"><b><i>RDL 2010/01</i></b></a><b><i>,
and </i></b><a href="52ce3983-2bfc-4e72-9359-42aaf5fe4509.html"><b><i>RDL 2016/01</i></b></a></p>

<p>The <b>Page.PageFooter</b> element specifies a footer that
is rendered at the bottom of each rendered <a href="b5e525d5-00d6-4e1a-8813-55f327da6b4c.html">Page</a> in RDL 2008/01
or at the bottom of <a href="96c3d25f-d8ce-4fe4-ab03-592edaa4a1da.html">ReportSection</a>
in RDL 2010/01 and RDL 2016/01. The <b>Page.PageFooter</b> element is
optional. This element is of type <a href="afff0921-7d95-4216-8f28-635c67d539d8.html">PageSection</a>.</p>
