<p><b><i>Applies to </i></b><a href="3428e690-a348-4ec7-8a6a-8efb42d2cdee.html"><b><i>RDL 2010/01</i></b></a><b><i>
and </i></b><a href="52ce3983-2bfc-4e72-9359-42aaf5fe4509.html"><b><i>RDL 2016/01</i></b></a></p>

<p>The <b>MapDataRegion</b> element provides a <a href="b2482b3f-74ab-4ca8-a9e5-c07955011743.html#gt_173464f7-c0e8-4181-941a-f7df6725be5a">map</a> <a href="b2482b3f-74ab-4ca8-a9e5-c07955011743.html#gt_c2e17144-2268-49d2-ba7c-493771d529b1">polygon layer</a>, a map <a href="b2482b3f-74ab-4ca8-a9e5-c07955011743.html#gt_8cc76ec5-c7e5-4806-a701-4487f95363d0">point layer</a>, or a map <a href="b2482b3f-74ab-4ca8-a9e5-c07955011743.html#gt_d18f341f-9a11-41e7-bc17-fa40808259cc">line layer</a> with analytical
data after it applies filtering and/or grouping to the layer. </p>
