and </i></b><a href="52ce3983-2bfc-4e72-9359-42aaf5fe4509.htm"><b><i>RDL 2016/01</i></b></a></p>

<p>The <b>ChartCustomPaletteColors</b> element specifies an
ordered list of colors to use for the custom palette of a <b>Chart</b>. This
element MUST contain at least one <a href="318caece-f3b8-4e2c-9e79-c67f343f9296.htm">ChartCustomPaletteColors.ChartCustomPaletteColor</a>
element.</p>
