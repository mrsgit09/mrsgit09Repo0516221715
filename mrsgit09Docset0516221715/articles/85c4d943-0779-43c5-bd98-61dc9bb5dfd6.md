<p><b>nid (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> Local
NID of the subnode. This NID is guaranteed to be unique only within the parent
node. </p>

<p><b>bidData (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> The
BID of the data block associated with the subnode.</p>

<p><b>bidSub (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> If
nonzero, the BID of the subnode of this subnode.</p>
