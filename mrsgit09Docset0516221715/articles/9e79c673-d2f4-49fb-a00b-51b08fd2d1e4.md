<p><b>nid (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> The key
NID value to the next-level child block. This NID is only unique within the
parent node. The NID is extended to 8 bytes in order for Unicode PST files to
follow the general convention of 8-byte indices (see section <a href="28fb2116-0998-4485-9844-9711b95603ba.htm">2.2.2.7.7.4</a> for details).</p>

<p><b>bid (Unicode: 8 bytes; ANSI: 4 bytes):</b> The BID
of the SLBLOCK.</p>
