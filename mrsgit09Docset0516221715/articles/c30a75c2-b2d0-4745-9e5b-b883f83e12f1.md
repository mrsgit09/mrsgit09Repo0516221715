is similar to Deleting a BTH entry in section <a href="21d49035-3818-4a4c-bc54-0e271cb9ce81.htm">2.6.2.2.4</a>, except when the

subnode (section <a href="30652aac-85b6-4da0-aa69-d88191f30a87.htm">2.6.1.2.6</a>).</p>
